ABOUT

Hi! Welcome to the about page lol. I just thought I'd give out a bit of the backstory of this website. So as you already know, this website was created by yours truly, Edwin Bartlett. I made it all from scratch, using just a simple text editor. The project came to mind when I was thinking of what to give you for your birthday, last June. I was so floored by all the artworks you made and showed me that I got inspired to do better and express myself more.

For the whole summer, I've been slowly studying web development and data science on my free time. The thing is, I haven't actually put much of my knowledge into practice. Somewhere along the line, I was planning to make a website but I didn't know what for. And then you came out, tricked me with the gallery website, and showcased your art to me. At that point, I thought, let's make it a reality!

And here we are! This website is dedicated all to you, Junnah. It's not the best but I hope you like it. It was actually originally going to be some sort of a puzzle game, but I decided to get straight to the point instead. It took me around a month in total. I did all the planning and layouting on June, but I only started to make real progress on August. What can I say, I'm just genuinely proud of this and I want to thank you for inspiring me to make it! I learned a lot from this, and I got to put my knowlede to the test. I might add some more content or new feautures in the future, but no promises! But one thing's for sure, I'm gonna make more beautiful projects like this.
